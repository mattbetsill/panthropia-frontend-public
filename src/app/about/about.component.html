About PANTHROPIA Overview Panthropia is a tool geared to help collegiate organizations, students, and communities. Organizations post events with the goal of making information more publicly available in order to drive attendance. For example, a percentage night for a philanthropy can be seen by everyone and result in more attendees and more money raised. Users can submit attendance in the form of a photo that can then be viewed by their own organization as well as the organization that hosted the event. For Users Set Up Account To set up an account, click Login then Register in the upper right hand corner or Register User in the side menu. Fill out the fields and hit Register. Note: If you don't see your organization, that means your organization has not set up an account yet. Log in with your credentials and select your campus. Attendance Submission Try submitting an attendance record by clicking "See More" on an event. Choose a photo and press "Ok". Now, navigate to your account page by clicking on your username or initials located at the top right. You should now see an attendance record for that event. You can delete the record if necessary. Both your organization as well as the organization hosting the event can approve or reject your attendance. If you run into any problems with functionality, don't hesitate to report an issue in the footer! For Organizations Set Up Account To set up an account, request an Authorization Key on the Register Organization page. This page can be found in the side menu, located on the top left of the page. Once emailed an Authorization Key, create your organization account and log in. Add Organization to Campus In order to have your organization displayed in the organizations tab, you must edit your profile under the "Profile" tab. Create Event To create an event, click the "Create Event" tab and enter the information for your event. Archive Event To Archive an event, click the three dot menu on one of your events. Select Archive. This event will now only show up to you in your profile tab. Nobody other than you will have access to that event while it is archived. It is recommended to archive events once they have passed, to keep the organization's profile up to date, or after no more attendance can be submitted. Edit Event To edit an event, click the menu on one of your events and select edit. Edit the fields and submit. The event will now be updated on all timelines. Private Events To create a private event, simply edit an existing event and check the box for a private event. Select the organizations from the dropdown menu you want to see the event. Your organization will always be able to see a private event. Event Types There are three event types: Philanthropy, Rush, and Other. Choose the one that best matches your event. Deleting Events To delete an event, click the menu icon on the event you created. Click delete. Keep in mind, this cannot be undone and you will lose access to the attendance records for the event. Attendance Data The "Attendance Data" tab has two modes: My Events and My Organization. My Events shows the attendance records of everyone who showed up to the event and submitted attendance. My Organization shows all of the events members of your organization have shown up to. In the "Show More" area of the attendance records there are two grids of data. The first grid is the grid of people who attended, and the second grid shows individuals from your organization who didn't attend the event. In both modes, attendance records may be accepted or rejected. Proof of attendance is also provided with each record by clicking the "view proof" button. To get past events on the Attendance Data page, select a date prior to the event's date. If you encounter any issues, please report it in the footer!
